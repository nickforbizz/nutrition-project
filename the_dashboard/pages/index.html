HOW TO USE THE SITE

  1. Visit The Recomendations PAGE To Fill Your Simple Data e.g "height, weight, ..."
  2. After filling the Form the system will suggest which food type you should be taking, you can chose the ones you like and they will be added to your favorite list
  3. If some food types arent Available, navigate to our Nutrition Information PAGE Where dozens of food are there, Click food to add to favorite
  4. Our Tables Contain search box from which you can search any food you want.
  5. The progress of your diet will be shown on our CHART PAGE, and there you can see if our system has offered some help.
